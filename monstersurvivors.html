Battle endless waves of monsters in this thrilling survival adventure!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game that puts your skills and strategy to the ultimate test. Face waves of increasingly challenging monsters while collecting power-ups and upgrading your character's abilities.

Key Features

  • Endless Gameplay

    Experience non-stop action with endless waves of unique monsters

  • Character Development

    Upgrade your abilities and become stronger as you progress

  • Dynamic Combat

    Fast-paced battles with various enemy types and attack patterns

  • Power-ups

    Collect various power-ups to enhance your survival chances

How to Play

Use your keyboard to move your character and avoid enemies. Collect experience points to level up and choose new abilities. Survive as long as possible while defeating endless waves of monsters.

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Choose power-ups wisely to create powerful combinations
